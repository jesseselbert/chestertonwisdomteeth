How Much Does Wisdom Teeth Removal Cost in Chesterton?

The price depends on whether the teeth are erupted or impacted, whether sedation is involved, and whether the estimate includes imaging and follow-up care. Patients comparing quotes should look at the whole surgical visit, not just the extraction line item. Often that quote comes after a general dentist identifies the problem and an oral surgeon confirms the surgical plan.

Directional planning ranges

Scenario Estimated range
Four erupted wisdom teeth $510 to $1,200
Four impacted teeth with IV sedation $1,120 to $2,660
Consultation and imaging allowance $140 to $310

What changes the fee

  • Impaction level and whether bone removal is needed
  • How many teeth come out in the same visit
  • Local anesthesia versus IV sedation
  • Imaging, follow-up visits, and any pathology fees
  • Insurance benefits and financing structure

How to compare two quotes

Ask whether each quote includes the panoramic X-ray, sedation, and postoperative care. A low extraction fee can still become a higher total if the rest of the surgical visit is broken out separately.

Best next step for real pricing

The most accurate number comes after imaging, exam, and a discussion of sedation options. That is especially true for impacted teeth.
